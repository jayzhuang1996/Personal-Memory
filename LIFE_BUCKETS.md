# The Biographer's Target Data (Life Buckets)

To build a true "Documentary of Jay," the AI must progressively extract data across these highly specific human dimensions, leaving no stone unturned over the years.

## 1. Origins & Family Dynamics
- **Ancestry & Heritage:** Where did the family originate? What are the stories generationally passed down?
- **Parental Relationships:** How did the relationship with parents evolve from childhood to adulthood? What traits were inherited or actively rejected?
- **Childhood Environment:** The physical spaces of youth. What did the childhood home feel like, and how did it dictate early behaviors?

## 2. Defining Trajectories (The Pivot Points)
- **The Crucible Moments:** What were the deepest failures or darkest periods, and what exact survival mechanics were learned from them?
- **Serendipity & Luck:** Encounters with strangers, mentors, or out-of-the-blue opportunities that drastically altered the life path.
- **Lost Passions:** Things Jay loved deeply but abandoned. Why were they let go?

## 3. Human Connection & Social Fabric
- **The Inner Circle:** Who are the 3-5 closest entities? What holds these bonds together?
- **Betrayals & Fallout:** Friendships or partnerships that ended. What was the autopsy of the failure?
- **Romantic & Emotional Philosophy:** How does Jay view partnership, vulnerability, and trust?

## 4. Internal Ideologies (The "Who Am I?")
- **Mortality & Legacy:** What is the underlying drive? Fear of running out of time, or desire to leave a mark?
- **Relationship with Money & Status:** How is success quantified internally versus externally?
- **Vices & Escapism:** How does the brain decompress when under maximum stress?

## 5. The Physical Reality
- **Relationship with the Body:** Health, fitness, limits of endurance. 
- **Geographic Anchors:** Which city/place makes Jay feel most alive, and which place feels most draining?

## 6. Daily Mindscape (The Present)
- **Current Obsessions:** What is dominating Jay's brain loops *this week* outside of work?
- **Fears of the Future:** What gives Jay anxiety about the next 5 years?
