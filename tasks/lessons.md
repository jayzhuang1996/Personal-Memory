# Lessons Learned

## 1. UX Parsing & Workflow Assumptions
**Mistake:** Assuming the user wanted a passive, standard "note-taking" RAG pipeline (dropping files into a folder) when they explicitly referred to a "Brain/Memory" referencing Karpathy. I was too rigid and focused on engineering documentation rather than personal history.
**Correction:** When the user asks for a "Brain", "Memory", or "Documentary of my life", they want an **Active Interview paradigm**. 
**Rule:** Design systems where the AI is *proactive* (drafting questions, prompting the user) rather than *reactive* (waiting for the user to upload a file). The AI should act as the biographer, and the user's only job is to speak (voice dictation).
